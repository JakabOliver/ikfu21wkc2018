The venue - Tüskecsarnok

Tüskecsarnok is one of the most modern arena of Hungary that is spectacular from outside. The 71 × 42 metre arena was opened in November 2014 and excellent for domestic and international sports events. Capacity: 3200

Address: 1117 Budapest, Magyar tudósok körútja 2.

Public transport

  • Tram 4 and 6: Petőfi híd, budai hídfő station
  • Metro line 4: Újbuda-központ station
  • Bus 153 and 212: Petőfi híd, budai hídfő station
  • Bus 33: Budafoki út station

Parking
2 levels for 435 cars
